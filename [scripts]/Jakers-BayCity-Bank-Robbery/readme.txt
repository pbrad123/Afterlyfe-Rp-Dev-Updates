#Install

EASY INSTALL DRAG AND DROP!

QB-Target already exported.

1) Drag and drop `Jakers-BayCity Bank-Robbery` into your server resources & ensure,
2) Add the Sounds to your interact-sound\client\html\sounds
3) Add the `BayCityBank.lua` to your nui door configs
4) Restart your server
